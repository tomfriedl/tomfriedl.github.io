Thanks for taking the time to take a look at my work. I'm a senior at American University in Washington, DC, majoring in Journalism with a minor in Philosophy. I have a broad interest in media production, particularly in the areas of graphic design and photography. Extra-curricular interests include labor politics and punk music. I also volunteer on weekends with DC Food Not Bombs, a vegan mutual-aid organization which works to redistribute surplus meals and produce to the homeless.
